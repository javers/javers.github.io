<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="wl-prose-subtitle">JaVers Pro Waiting List</p>
<p class="wl-prose-updated">Last updated: May 19, 2026</p>

<h2>What data we collect</h2>
<p>When you join the JaVers Pro waiting list, we collect:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Your <strong>email address</strong> (required)</li>
    <li>Your <strong>selected pricing tier</strong> preference (Team or Enterprise)</li>
    <li>Optional <strong>feedback or comments</strong> you provide in the form</li>
</ul>

<h2>How we use your data</h2>
<p>We use your information solely to:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Send you updates about JaVers Pro availability and early-access offers</li>
    <li>Understand demand across pricing tiers to guide product development</li>
</ul>
<p>We will <strong>not</strong> sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.</p>

<h2>Third-party services</h2>
<p>We use the following third-party services to operate the waiting list:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>MailerLite</strong> — stores your email address and manages mailing list subscriptions. See <a href="https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">MailerLite's Privacy Policy</a>.</li>
    <li><strong>Cloudflare Turnstile</strong> — provides CAPTCHA verification to prevent spam submissions. Cloudflare may collect technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type) during verification. See <a href="https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cloudflare's Privacy Policy</a>.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Data retention</h2>
<p>Your data is retained in our MailerLite mailing list for as long as you remain subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send you.</p>

<h2>Your rights</h2>
<p>You have the right to:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Unsubscribe</strong> from the mailing list at any time via the unsubscribe link in our emails</li>
    <li><strong>Request deletion</strong> of your personal data from our systems</li>
    <li><strong>Request access</strong> to the personal data we hold about you</li>
</ul>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at <a href="mailto:contact@javers.org">contact@javers.org</a>.</p>
